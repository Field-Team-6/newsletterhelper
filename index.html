BLURBINATOR
Multi-Tool to Help Build the Field Team 6 Weekly

🗳️ Voter Drive List Generator

▼
Fetches all upcoming in-person voter registration drives from Mobilize and formats them ready to paste into your newsletter. Automatically excludes trainings.

📬 Postcarding States Generator

▼
Logs into postcards.fieldteam6.org and reads the current list of active postcarding states — formatted and ready to paste into the newsletter.

🔍 Proof Link Checker

▼
Paste the proof newsletter URL and hit Check — the app fetches the HTML automatically. Every RSVP link is verified against the actual Mobilize event (title, day of week, date, time & timezone). All other links are checked for reachability.

✏️ Proofread Newsletter

▼
Uses AI to proofread the newsletter for typos, capitalization errors, profanity, double spaces, hyphenation, name spelling, hotlink punctuation, and font consistency. Auto-fills from the Proof Link Checker URL if already entered.
